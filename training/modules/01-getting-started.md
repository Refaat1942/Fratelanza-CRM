# Module 01 — Getting Started / البداية

> **Duration:** 30 minutes | **Audience:** All staff
> **Route:** `/login` → Dashboard

---

## 1. Logging in / تسجيل الدخول

1. Open your pharmacy link: `https://[your-name].fratelanza.com`
2. Enter **username** and **password** provided by your manager
3. On first login, the system asks you to **change your password** — choose something you'll remember
4. Click **Login / تسجيل الدخول**

> ⚠️ Never share your password. Each person should have their own account.

---

## 2. Switching language / تغيير اللغة

- Look for the **EN / ع** toggle in the top bar
- Click it to switch between English and Arabic
- The entire interface flips to RTL (right-to-left) in Arabic mode
- **Recommendation:** Use Arabic for daily work; switch to English only if needed

---

## 3. Main navigation / التنقل في القائمة

The sidebar on the left (or right in Arabic) shows your available modules:

| Menu item | Arabic | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| Dashboard | لوحة التحكم | Overview: revenue, clients, tasks |
| Products | المنتجات | Drug & supply inventory |
| CRM / Clients | العملاء | Customer records |
| Invoices | الفواتير | Sales invoices |
| Suppliers | الموردين | Vendor directory |
| Purchase Orders | أوامر الشراء | Restocking orders |
| Finance | المالية | Income & expenses |
| Reports | التقارير | Analytics |
| Notifications | الإشعارات | Alerts & updates |
| Settings | الإعدادات | Admin only: users, branding |

> You only see modules your manager enabled for your account. If something is missing, ask your admin.

---

## 4. General vs Medical workspace / مساحة العمل

If your pharmacy has the medical module enabled, you'll see a **workspace switcher** at the top of the sidebar:

- **General** — daily pharmacy operations (products, invoices, suppliers)
- **Medical** — medicine formulary, prescriptions, patient records

Most counter staff work in **General** all day. Pharmacists switch to **Medical** when handling prescriptions.

---

## 5. Notifications / الإشعارات

- Click the **bell icon** 🔔 in the top bar
- Shows low-stock alerts, task assignments, and system messages
- Unread count appears as a red badge

---

## 6. Branch selector / اختيار الفرع

If your pharmacy has multiple branches:

- A **branch dropdown** appears in the top bar
- Select your branch — all data (products, invoices) filters to that branch
- Admins can view all branches or switch between them

---

## 7. Logging out / تسجيل الخروج

- Click your **name/avatar** in the top bar
- Select **Logout / تسجيل الخروج**
- Always log out on shared computers

---

## Practice exercise / تمرين عملي

1. Log in with your credentials
2. Switch language to Arabic
3. Open Dashboard — note the KPI cards
4. Click through: Products → Invoices → CRM (just look, don't edit)
5. Check notifications bell
6. Log out

---

## Troubleshooting / حل المشاكل

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| "Invalid credentials" | Check username/password caps. Ask manager to reset. |
| Page shows "internal error" | Wait 1 minute and retry. Contact support if persists. |
| Module missing from sidebar | Your account may not have permission. Ask manager. |
| Can't change password | Password must meet minimum length. Try a longer one. |
