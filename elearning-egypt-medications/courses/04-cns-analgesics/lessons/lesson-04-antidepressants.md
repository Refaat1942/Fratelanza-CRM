## Learning objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

1. Explain **SSRI/SNRI mechanisms**
2. Identify **Cipralex, Seroxat, Cymbalta**
3. Counsel **2-4 week onset**
4. Manage **discontinuation syndrome**
5. Screen **SSRI + tramadol** interaction

---

## 1. SSRIs Egypt
| Generic | Brand | Dose |
|---|---|---|
| Escitalopram | Cipralex | 10-20mg OD |
| Sertraline | Lustral | 50-200mg |
| Paroxetine | Seroxat | 20mg OD |
| Fluoxetine | Prozac | 20mg OD |

## 2. SNRIs
Venlafaxine (Efexor), Duloxetine (Cymbalta) — also pain indications.

**Counseling (Arabic):** "مضاد الاكتئاب محتاج 2-4 أسابيع. متوقفوش فجأة. لو أفكار سيئة تزيد، كلم الدكتور فوراً."

## 3. Scenario
> Stopped Seroxat after 3 days — dizziness, electric shocks. Explain discontinuation; gradual taper needed.

---

## Key takeaways

> 📌 **Key Takeaways — EG-MED-04-L04**
> - Cipralex widely used Egypt
> - 2-4 week onset essential counseling
> - Taper SSRIs — never abrupt
> - SSRI + tramadol danger
> - Seroxat avoid pregnancy
> - Cymbalta for pain+depression
