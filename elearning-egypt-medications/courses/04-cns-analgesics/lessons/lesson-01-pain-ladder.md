## Learning objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

1. Describe **nociceptive vs neuropathic** pain
2. Apply **WHO analgesic ladder**
3. Identify **red flag pain**
4. Counsel on **pain expectations** in Arabic
5. Know when to **refer**

---

## 1. Pain types
Nociceptive: tissue injury. Neuropathic: nerve damage — burning, shooting.

## 2. WHO ladder
| Step | Drugs (Egypt) |
|---|---|
| 1 Mild | Cetal, Brufen |
| 2 Moderate | + Tramal (tramadol) |
| 3 Severe | Morphine (hospital) |
| Adjuvant | Amitriptyline, Lyrica |

**Counseling (Arabic):** "الألم العصبي محتاج أدوية مختلفة. متزودش الجرعة من نفسك — اتبع الدكتور."

## 3. Scenario
> Thunderclap headache — refer emergency immediately.

---

## Key takeaways

> 📌 **Key Takeaways — EG-MED-04-L01**
> - WHO ladder guides stepwise analgesia
> - Neuropathic pain needs adjuvants
> - Red flags need urgent referral
> - Tramadol step 2 — controlled Egypt
> - OTC paracetamol/NSAIDs step 1
