## 1. Lipid physiology & targets

### 1.1 Atherosclerosis mechanism

```
↑ LDL-C → Intimal deposition → Foam cells → Plaque → CV events
Statins: ↓ HMG-CoA reductase → ↓ hepatic cholesterol synthesis → ↑ LDL receptors → ↓ LDL-C 30–50%
```

**Evidence:** CTT Collaboration meta-analysis — each 1 mmol/L LDL reduction → 22% relative reduction in major vascular events. WOSCOPS, 4S, LIPID trials established mortality benefit.

### 1.2 Lipid targets (ESC 2021 / Egyptian practice)

| Risk category | LDL-C target |
|---|---|
| Very high risk (established ASCVD, DM with TOD) | **<55 mg/dL** (<1.4 mmol/L) |
| High risk (DM, CKD, familial hypercholesterolemia) | **<70 mg/dL** (<1.8 mmol/L) |
| Moderate risk | **<100 mg/dL** (<2.6 mmol/L) |
| Low risk | **<116 mg/dL** (<3.0 mmol/L) |

**Egyptian context:** Dyslipidemia prevalence ~40% adults; low statin adherence due to cost and muscle symptom fears.

### 2.1 Potency comparison

| Statin | LDL reduction at starting dose |
|---|---|
| Rosuvastatin 10mg | ~45–50% |
| Atorvastatin 20mg | ~40–45% |
| Simvastatin 40mg | ~35–40% |
| Pravastatin 40mg | ~30–35% |

### 2.2 Contraindications & interactions

| Contraindication | Interaction |
|---|---|
| Active liver disease, pregnancy | Gemfibrozil + simvastatin → rhabdomyolysis |
| Unexplained persistent ↑ transaminases | Amlodipine → limit simvastatin to 20mg |
| | Clarithromycin, azole antifungals → ↑ statin levels |
| | Grapefruit juice → ↑ simvastatin/atorvastatin |

**Counseling script (Arabic):**

> "الستاتين بياخد بالليل أو أي وقت ثابت يومياً. ممكن تحس بألم عضلات خفيف في الأول — لو الألم شديد أو مع بول غامق، وقف الدواء وروح للدكتور فوراً. متاخدش الجريب فروت مع سيمفاستاتين. الكوليسترول محتاج 6–8 أسابيع عشان تشوف النتيجة في التحليل."

## 4. Pharmacy practice scenario

### Scenario 1.5 — Statin muscle pain
> *Patient on atorvastatin 40mg (Ator, Amoun) for 2 months. Reports bilateral thigh aching. Taking erythromycin for respiratory infection.*

**Response:**
1. Recognize statin-associated muscle symptoms (SAMS) + macrolide interaction
2. Advise immediate physician contact — check CK if symptoms severe
3. Counsel: do not stop without physician but report erythromycin use
4. Suggest physician may switch to pravastatin or reduce dose
5. Dispense no additional pain NSAIDs without checking renal function and BP

## Key takeaways

> 📌 **Key Takeaways — Lesson 1.5**
> - Atorvastatin (Liptior/Ator) and rosuvastatin (Crestor) dominate Egyptian market
> - Amoun and Memphis produce affordable atorvastatin/rosuvastatin generics
> - LDL target <55 mg/dL for very high-risk Egyptian patients with ASCVD
> - Simvastatin max 20mg with amlodipine — common Egyptian HTN+dyslipidemia combo
> - SAMS management: rule out interactions, try alternate statin or lower dose
> - Ezetimibe (Ezetrol) available as add-on when LDL not at target
