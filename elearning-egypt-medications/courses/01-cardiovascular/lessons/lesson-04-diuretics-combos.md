## 1. Diuretic mechanisms

### 1.1 Classification

| Class | Site | Examples (Egypt) | Key effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Thiazide-like** | DCT | HCTZ, indapamide (Natrilix) | Na⁺/Cl⁻ excretion; ↓ BP |
| **Loop** | Thick ascending limb | Furosemide (Lasix), torasemide | Potent diuresis |
| **K-sparing** | Collecting duct | Spironolactone (Aldactone), amiloride | ↓ K⁺ loss; anti-aldosterone |

```
Thiazides: Initial ↓ plasma volume → long-term ↓ peripheral resistance
Loop diuretics: Inhibit Na-K-2Cl cotransporter → rapid diuresis
Spironolactone: Aldosterone antagonist → mortality benefit in HFrEF (RALES trial)
```

**Evidence:** ALLHAT — chlorthalidone equivalent to amlodipine. RALES — spironolactone reduced HF mortality by 30%.

### 1.4 Contraindications & interactions

| Diuretic | Contraindications | Key interactions |
|---|---|---|
| Thiazides | Anuria, severe renal impairment (eGFR <30) | ↑ lithium toxicity; ↓ hypoglycemic effect of sulfonylureas |
| Loop | Anuria, severe hypokalemia | Ototoxicity with aminoglycosides |
| Spironolactone | Hyperkalemia, Addison's | ACE-I/ARB → dangerous hyperkalemia; avoid K+ supplements |
| All | Gout (thiazides ↑ uric acid) | NSAIDs ↓ diuretic efficacy |

**Counseling script (Arabic):**

> "مدر البول بيزود التبول — خده الصبح عشان متتعبش بالليل. لو حسيت بدوخة، قوم ببطء. راقب علامات نقص البوتاسيوم: تعب عضلات، ضعف، عدم انتظام ضربات القلب. متاخدش مكملات بوتاسيوم من غير تحليل واستشارة دكتور."

- **Adherence:** One pill vs two — critical in Egypt where polypharmacy is common
- **Cost:** Egyptian generics often cheaper than two separate branded products
- **Step therapy:** Matches ESC/NICE algorithm directly

## 3. Pharmacy practice scenario

### Scenario 1.4 — Hypokalemia on HCTZ
> *Patient on Co-Tareg (losartan/HCTZ) for 6 months. Complains of muscle cramps and fatigue. On ibuprofen (Brufen) daily for knee pain.*

**Response:**
1. Recognize hypokalemia risk: HCTZ + possible NSAID interaction
2. Advise physician review — serum K+ and creatinine needed
3. Counsel to stop unsupervised ibuprofen — worsens BP and K+ balance
4. Do NOT sell KCl supplements without prescription and K+ level
5. Explain that ARB (losartan) partially protects against thiazide hypokalemia

## Key takeaways

> 📌 **Key Takeaways — Lesson 1.4**
> - HCTZ and indapamide (Natrilix) are key thiazide-like agents in Egypt — very affordable
> - Lasix (furosemide) from EIPICO is the standard loop diuretic
> - Spironolactone (Aldactone) — mortality benefit in HF; watch K+ with ACE-I/ARB
> - Exforge, Co-Tareg, Co-Diovan — major branded FDCs; Egyptian generics widely available
> - Triple therapy: ARB/ACE + CCB + diuretic for resistant HTN
> - NSAIDs antagonize diuretics and raise BP — common Egyptian interaction
