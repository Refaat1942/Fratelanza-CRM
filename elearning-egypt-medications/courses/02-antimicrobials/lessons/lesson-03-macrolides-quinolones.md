## 1. Macrolides

### 1.1 Mechanism & evidence

Bind 50S ribosomal subunit → inhibit protein synthesis. Azithromycin achieves high tissue concentrations with short courses.

| Generic | Brand (Egypt) | Manufacturer | Dosing | Approx. EGP |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Azithromycin** | Zithromax, Azithrocin | Pfizer / Pharco, Memphis | 500mg OD × 3 days | 80–200 |
| **Clarithromycin** | Klacid, Clabact | Abbott / generic | 500mg BD × 7 days | 150–350 |
| **Erythromycin** | Erythrocin | Limited | 250–500mg QDS | 40–100 |

**Indications:** Atypical pneumonia, strep pharyngitis (pen allergy), H. pylori triple therapy (with PPI + amoxicillin).

**Interactions:** ↑ statin myopathy risk; ↑ warfarin INR; QT prolongation with amiodarone (Cordarone).

## 2. Fluoroquinolones (AWaRe WATCH)

| Generic | Brand (Egypt) | Dosing | Key risks |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ciprofloxacin** | Ciprobay, Ciprodar | 500mg BD | Tendon rupture, QT, CNS effects |
| **Levofloxacin** | Tavanic, Levoxin | 500mg OD | Respiratory infections |
| **Moxifloxacin** | Avalox | 400mg OD | QT prolongation |

**Egyptian resistance:** E. coli fluoroquinolone resistance 50–70% in many hospital surveys — avoid empiric cipro for uncomplicated cystitis.

| Contraindication | Interaction |
|---|---|
| Children <18 (cartilage damage) | Antacids ↓ absorption — separate 2h |
| Pregnancy | Corticosteroids ↑ tendon rupture risk |
| History FQ tendon disorder | Warfarin ↑ INR |

## 3. Tetracyclines

| Generic | Brand (Egypt) | Dosing | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Doxycycline** | Vibramycin, Doxymycin | 100mg BD | Acne, atypicals, malaria prophylaxis |
| **Minocycline** | Minocin (limited) | 100mg BD | Acne |
| **Tetracycline** | Generic | 250–500mg QDS | Cheaper; more GI upset |

**Counseling script (Arabic):**

> "سيبروباي بنستخدمه بحذر في مصر لأن بكتيريا البول مقاومة ليه كتير. لو حسيت ألم في أوتار الركبة أو الكاحل، وقف الدواء وروح للدكتور. الدوكسيسيكلين ممنوع في الحمل والأطفال الصغيرين، ويسبب حساسية شمس — استخدم واقي."

## 4. Pharmacy scenario

### Scenario 2.3 — Fluoroquinolone for UTI
> *Prescription: Ciprobay 500mg BD × 7 days for uncomplicated cystitis in healthy 28-year-old woman.*

**Response:**
1. Stewardship concern — prefer nitrofurantoin or cephalexin if susceptibility unknown
2. If dispensed: counsel tendon pain, hydration, avoid antacids
3. Warn: stop if joint/tendon pain; report to physician
4. No FQ in pregnancy — confirm not pregnant

---

## Key takeaways

> 📌 **Key Takeaways — EG-MED-02-L03**
> - Zithromax 3-day course — widely used in Egypt for respiratory infections
> - Ciprobay — AWaRe Watch; high E. coli resistance limits empiric UTI use
> - FQs contraindicated children <18 and pregnancy
> - Doxycycline — photosensitivity and teeth staining in children <8
> - Macrolide + statin — monitor for myopathy
> - Klacid component of Egyptian H. pylori eradication regimens
