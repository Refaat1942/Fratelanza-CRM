## 📦 The 5 packages / الباقات الخمسة

### 1. Starter / المبتدئ
**EGP 499 / month** — أو **5,499 / السنة** (وفّر شهرين)

- Up to **3 users**
- Dashboard, Tasks, CRM (Clients), Notifications, Reports

- Email support, response within 48 hours

### 2. Business / الأعمال ⭐ Most popular
**EGP 999 / month** — أو **10,999 / السنة** (وفّر شهرين)

Everything in Starter, plus:
- Up to **10 users**
- Finance, Team/HR, Products/Inventory, Suppliers, Purchase Orders, Invoicing, Rentals
- **Multi-branch** support (per-branch reports)

> **Best for:** retail stores, agencies, rental companies, multi-branch SMBs.
> **مناسب لـ:** محلات التجزئة، الوكالات، شركات الإيجارات، الشركات متعددة الفروع.

### 3. Clinic / العيادة
**EGP 1,499 / month** — أو **16,499 / السنة**

Everything in Business, plus:
- **Medical module**: Patients, Appointments, Visits, Procedures catalog, Medical Invoices, Treatment Plans, Medical Reports
- Up to **15 users** (doctors + receptionists)
- WhatsApp appointment reminders
- Medical reports auto-feed into Finance — no double entry

> **Best for:** single doctors, polyclinics, medical centers.
> **مناسب لـ:** الأطباء المنفردين، المجمعات الطبية، المراكز الطبية.

### 4. Dental Clinic / عيادة الأسنان
**EGP 1,799 / month** — أو **19,799 / السنة**

Everything in Clinic, plus:
- **Dental module**: 32-tooth interactive FDI chart per patient, dental procedure catalog (cleaning, filling, crown, root canal, etc.), tooth-specific treatment log with materials
- Pre-seeded dental procedure catalog (11 common procedures, editable)

> **Best for:** dental clinics, orthodontists, dental groups.
> **مناسب لـ:** عيادات الأسنان، أخصائيي التقويم، المجموعات الطبية للأسنان.

---

### 5. Enterprise / المؤسسات
**Starting at EGP 4,999 / month** — تواصل معنا للتفاصيل

Everything above, plus:
- **Unlimited users**
- Dedicated database server (not shared)
- White-label option (your brand, your domain)
- On-premise installation on customer's own VPS
- Custom features and integrations
- 1-hour SLA, dedicated account manager
- Quarterly disaster-recovery drill

> **Best for:** hospitals, large dental chains, multi-location enterprises, anyone with data-residency requirements.
